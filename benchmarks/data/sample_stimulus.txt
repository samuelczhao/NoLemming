Company X reports Q4 2025 earnings. Revenue of $25.3 billion beats analyst estimates of $24.1 billion. Earnings per share of $2.18 versus expected $1.95. The company raised full-year guidance citing strong demand across all product lines. Management highlighted 35% year-over-year growth in the cloud division and announced a $10 billion share buyback program. Analysts noted the beat was driven primarily by better-than-expected margins rather than top-line growth alone.
